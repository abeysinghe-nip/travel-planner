Unwavering assistance for your travel emergencies

In Sri Lanka, both visitors to the country and locals can reach out for immediate assistance in case of a health emergency or when in danger. The following numbers are available for emergency access and can be contacted nationwide through all telephone and mobile networks. Even with low signals or low credit, many mobile services prioritize emergency calls to ensure help is always within reach. Please note that while emergency services are readily available in town centers and suburbs, police stations and hospitals also provide emergency care in rural areas.
